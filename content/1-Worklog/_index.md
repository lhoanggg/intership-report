**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Onboarding and AWS Fundamentals](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS IAM, VPC Architecture & EC2 Deployment](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Deploying and Managing AWS Services with CLI](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AWS Deployment – Scaling – Monitoring – DNS Management](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Strengthening Full-Stack Skills and Exploring NoSQL Databases](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Practicing AWS CLI, S3, CloudFront, and Lambda](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Advanced AWS Practice and Lab Knowledge Consolidation](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Reviewing Learned Knowledge and Midterm Preparation](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Final Project Preparation and Back-End/React Review](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Backend Implementation, Dataset Creation, and DynamoDB Setup](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Writing APIs and Updating DynamoDB for Final Project](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Writing APIs and Updating DynamoDB for Final Project](1.12-week12/)
